architecture and designers festival
2023

The Architects and Designers Festival brings together a community of experts, academics, and activists in the field of architecture and design in a different location every two years. This year, we aim to expand our guest list to include organizations and individuals interested in collaborating with us on joint projects that promote the advancement of the industry, free expression, and open knowledge. If you're actively involved in the world of architecture and design - advocates for open-source software, design enthusiasts, professionals from galleries, libraries, museums, archives, government bodies, foundations, lawyers, and activists - we invite you to join us this year to work together in creating a more robust and dynamic community.
